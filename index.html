Full time

Current work I'm involved with is sponsored by DoD and other federal agencies. I'm the project lead and a full stack developer of the Propagation Modeling Website (PMW), which is a web based signal propagation analysis tool. The PMW parallelizes low level Fortran and C propagation models, such as the IRI, produces intermediate results, massages them, stores them and retrieves them.

I wrote a good portion of a Radio Frequency Coordination Portal (RFCP) for the winners of the 1695-1710 MHz spectrum auction. Particularly the user authentication, email services, and other administrative subsystems.

Most of this work is using .NET, ArcObjects (from Esri) and various other Microsoft frameworks, however I'm comfortable developing in Linux as well as OS X.
